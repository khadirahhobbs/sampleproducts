Pack City is known for curating the best-known-to-man bags for the everyday adventure. From backpacks, to shoulder bags, to purses, we hold them all

In the early 1900s, Peter Alexander Cormack, a barrel maker by trade, and his wife Annie made the journey from Wick, Scotland, to Canada. The couple settled in the small town of Pack City, whose population today is counted at 30 residents.

Founded in 2009 by brothers Jamie and Lyndon Cormack, Pack City Supply Co. adopted the name of the town where three generations of their family grew up. Based in Vancouver, Canada, Pack City Supply Co. is a design driven global accessories brand that produces quality products with a fine regard for detail.
